Sumo Battlebot

Description

Welcome! to our sumo battlebot website were you take a look at some of our sumo battlebots that were created by students and also some videos that were taken showing some of the battlebots that were done. Sumo Battle bot was a project done by DVC students which consisted of designing a special armor for your robot in order to survive the battle against other students robots. In this website you be able to see yourself some images and videos of the sumo battlebots created by juniors and seniors.
